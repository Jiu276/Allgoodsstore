Richard Haworth: Hotel Linen Experts Crafted in Britain Since 1876

When it comes to luxury hotel linen, one name stands above the rest in British manufacturing excellence: Richard Haworth. For nearly 150 years, this distinguished British company has been crafting premium linens that grace some of the world's finest establishments. From boutique hotels to grand resorts, Richard Haworth has become synonymous with quality, durability, and the quintessential British craftsmanship that the hospitality industry demands.

A Rich Heritage Dating Back to 1876

The story of Richard Haworth begins in the heart of Britain's textile heartland, where founder Richard Haworth established his vision of creating exceptional linens for the burgeoning hospitality industry. What started as a modest operation in 1876 has grown into one of Britain's most respected names in hotel textiles.

Throughout nearly 150 years of operation, the company has witnessed the evolution of the hospitality industry, adapting and innovating while staying true to its core values of quality and service. From the Victorian era through two world wars and into the modern age of boutique hotels and luxury resorts, Richard Haworth has remained at the forefront of linen excellence.

British Craftsmanship at Its Finest

What sets Richard Haworth apart is their unwavering commitment to British manufacturing. In an era where many companies have moved production overseas, Richard Haworth proudly maintains their production facilities in Britain, employing skilled artisans who bring decades of experience to every piece they create.
