Delivery & Shipping Showdown

Walmart+ Delivery

Same-Day Delivery: Available from local stores with $35 minimum. Delivery windows typically 1-2 hours. Powered by Walmart's own delivery network and third-party services.

Express Delivery: Get items in as little as 30 minutes for an additional $10 fee.

Coverage: Available to 80% of US households.

Our Experience: 4.5/5 - Fast and reliable

Target Circle 360 Delivery

Same-Day Delivery: Powered by Shipt, with $35 minimum. 1-hour delivery windows. Includes fresh groceries, alcohol, and household essentials.

Drive Up: Free curbside pickup in 2 hours (not exclusive to members).

Coverage: Available in all major metropolitan areas.

Our Experience: 4.0/5 - Good but limited rural coverage

The Verdict: Which Membership Wins?

Choose Walmart+ If You:

  • Drive regularly (fuel savings alone can pay for membership)
  • Shop for groceries weekly
  • Value speed and convenience over style
  • Live in suburban or rural areas
  • Want entertainment perks (Paramount+)
  • Prefer lower everyday prices

Choose Target Circle 360 If You:

  • Prioritize quality and style
  • Shop for home decor and clothing

Our Recommendation

For most shoppers, Walmart+ offers superior value, especially if you factor in fuel savings. The combination of free delivery, shipping with no minimum, and gas discounts typically saves members $400-600 annually. However, Target Circle 360 shines for style-conscious shoppers who value quality over quantity.

Consider trying both with their free trials: Walmart+ offers 30 days free, while Target Circle 360 provides a 7-day trial. Many savvy shoppers maintain both memberships, using each retailer's strengths to maximize savings.

Maximizing Your Membership Value

Walmart+ Tips

  • Fill up on Tuesdays for extra fuel savings
  • Use InHome delivery for ultimate convenience ($148/year)
  • Stack manufacturer coupons with member prices
  • Shop early morning for best delivery slots

Target Circle Tips

  • Combine Circle offers with RedCard 5% off
  • Use Drive Up for free same-day pickup
  • Shop clearance endcaps for 70% off deals
  • Time purchases with bonus gift card promotions

Bottom Line

Both Walmart+ and Target Circle 360 offer compelling benefits that can easily pay for themselves if used regularly. Walmart+ edges ahead with broader appeal and tangible fuel savings, while Target Circle 360 caters to a specific demographic that values style and shopping experience.
